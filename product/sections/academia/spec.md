# Academia & Local SEO (Strategic Authority)

## 1. Overview
**Goal:** Position Calvo Creativo/Guitar Curves not just as a store, but as a "Post-Op Recovery Institute".
**Strategy:** Capture top-of-funnel traffic through:
1.  **The Library (Academia):** High-value, medically reviewed guides (E-E-A-T).
2.  **Local Directory:** Programmatic landing pages for "Post-Op Massage [City]" to drive local intent traffic.

## 2. Core Features

### A. The Library (Blog)
*   **Concept:** "Medical Journals", not "Blog Posts".
*   **Design:** Clean, typography-focused reading experience (Medium-style).
*   **Key Content Types:**
    *   *The Recovery Manuals:* "How to pee with a faja", "Stage 1 vs Stage 2".
    *   *Surgeon Spotlight:* Interviews with plastic surgeons (Authority building).

### B. Local Specialist Directory (SEO Trap)
*   **Concept:** A directory of vetted providers (e.g., Lymphatic Massage Therapists) in key cities.
*   **SEO Utility:** Ranks for "Lymphatic Massage Miami", "Post-Op Care Los Angeles".
*   **Conversion:** "While you book your massage, get the faja your therapist recommends."

## 3. Data Requirements

### Content Schema
*   **Article:** Title, Author (Dr. vetted), Category, Read Time, Body (Markdown), Related Products.
*   **Provider:** Name, City, Service (Massage, Nurse), Rating, Recommended Products.
